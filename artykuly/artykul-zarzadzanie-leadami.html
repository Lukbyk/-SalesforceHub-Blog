How to Effectively Manage Leads in Salesforce

December 28, 2024

The Challenge of Lead Management

Learn best practices for lead management that will help you increase conversion and better organize your sales process. Discover practical tips for lead qualification, follow-up strategies, and using Salesforce views effectively.

Key takeaway: A well-managed lead pipeline is the foundation of predictable revenue.

1. Standardize Your Lead Qualification

Not all leads are created equal. Work with your team to define what a "qualified lead" means for your business. Use a simple framework like BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) and create custom fields in Salesforce to track this information.

2. Automate Follow-up Reminders

Never let a lead fall through the cracks. Use Salesforce Tasks or a Flow to automatically create a follow-up task for a sales rep a few days after a new lead is assigned. This ensures consistent engagement.

3. Leverage Custom List Views

Instead of looking at "All Open Leads," create targeted list views that help you prioritize. Examples include:

  • "My New Leads Today"
  • "Leads with No Activity in 7 Days"
  • "High-Priority Leads from a Recent Webinar"

These focused views reduce noise and help reps focus on the most important actions.
